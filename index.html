Research Interests

I'm a member of Sunlab on AI for healthcare, led by Prof. Jimeng Sun. My recent research focus is AI for clinical trial optimization. Before joining UIUC, I've worked on a variety of problems, including

  • Machine Learning. Information-principled interpretation and enhancement for representation learning.

  • NLP & Healthcare. Graph neural networks, lifelong learning, and relation extraction based on electronic health record (EHR), as well as their applications in healthcare.

  • Smart Building. AI for smart construction surveillance.
