Research Interests

I'm a member of Sunlab on AI for healthcare, led by Prof. Jimeng Sun. I am working on AI for clinical trials & healthcare, including several topics like:

  • Prediction. Flexible individual/trial outcome prediction with tabular/sequence model.

  • Generation. Synthetic EHR/EDC generation to boost AI for health/trial.

  • Optimization. In silico optimization of clinical trial protocols through retrieval, prediction, & generation.

  • Explanation. Provide trustworthy explanations to support decision-making in clinical trial design.
